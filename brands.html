Featured Brands

Bisleri

Bisleri was originally an Italian alcohol remedy drink created by 19th century inventor Felice Bisleri. In 1965, Italian doctor Cesari Rossi and Indian businessman Khushroo Suntook introduced Bisleri bottled water in India by setting up a factory in Thane.

Aquafina

Aquafina is an American brand of purified bottled water that is produced by PepsiCo, consisting of both unflavored and flavored water. The Aquafina brand name is also licensed for use on multiple skin care products, including lip balm and wrinkle cream.

Kinley

Kinley is an adult soft drink introduced in the 70s, bringing the international bitter flavor to mainland Europe. Today, through its unique propositions, it offers a balanced taste, inspired by the most beloved flavors from different cities worldwide.

Vedica

The perfect balance of naturally occurring Calcium, Magnesium, Sodium, Sulphates, and Bicarbonates lends this water, a distinctly sweet flavour without an aftertaste. Vedica has a high pH of 7.9 (high alkalinity and low acidity), and thus works as a natural antacid and a calming antidote for our stressful urban lives.

Bailey

Bailley has been the symbol of purity and is widely preferred as a source of safe drinking water. Packaged across India in 52 state-of-the-art bottling plants, Bailley caters to diverse consumer needs and occasions. It is available in various pack sizes like 250ml, 500ml, 1ltr, 2ltr PET, as well as 5ltr and 20ltr jar.
